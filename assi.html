PLACES TO VISIT IN SINDH

Dadu

some of the attractive places in dadu are: Gorakh Hill Station, Kirthar National Park, Nai Gaj, Darbar Karampur Sharif, Jamia Mosque (Khudabad)

Thatta

some of the attractive places in thatta are: Makli Hills, Shah jahan Mosque, Kenjhar lake, Sunway Lagoon, Hadero lake, Eidgah Makli

Hyderabad

some of the attractive places in Hyderabd are: Boulevard Mall, University of Sindh Campus, Sindh Museum, Mukhi House, Market Tower (Ghanta Ghar), Pakka Kila
